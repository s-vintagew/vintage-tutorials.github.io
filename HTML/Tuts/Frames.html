Frames

Frames are yet another important part of the HTML Document. They are what we call as body of the Web-page. In short we can say that whatever we display in HTML Body or Web-page body is a frame. Now use of frames comes to picture when we have to display one or more Web-page within a Web-page. For example this very page you are viewing. The side bar containing the HTML Tag list is a Web-page and this part where you are reading the text is yet another Web-page and the TOP and Bottom of the page with the logo is the parent Web-page that is containing the side and this web-page.
